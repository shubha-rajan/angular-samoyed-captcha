Meet Alice and Jamie.

Can you tell these two apart? The machine learning model we trained with Google Cloud AutoML can! Alice and Jamie's entire lives have been documented on camera by their dad, Doug. That's over 10 years worth of data that we were able to use to teach our model to tell these two apart! Our model was ultimately able to guess which dog is which with over 80% accuracy. Think you can do better? Try our captcha game below!

This project was built by Doug Mahugh and Shubha Rajan Arehalli, who work as Developer Programs Engineers on the Google Cloud Storage and Database team.
